About The Main Sequence

A different kind of consultant.

We are a boutique consulting practice built on the belief that the best work comes from clarity of purpose, depth of experience, and a genuine commitment to client outcomes.

How We Work

What you can count on.

How we work matters just as much as what we deliver. These principles shape every engagement, from how we think to how we execute.

01
Purpose
We start with your goals, not assumptions. Before recommending anything, we work to understand your business, your audience, and what success actually looks like — so we can focus on the problem that truly matters.
02
Partnership
We work alongside your team, not above it. You get shared ownership, open communication, and real collaboration throughout the process — not a handoff at the end.
03
Performance
Everything we do is built to drive results. If it can’t be measured, improved, or tied to impact, it doesn’t belong. We focus on work that performs and holds up over time.
04
Perspective
We bring experience from across industries, which helps us see patterns and opportunities others might miss. We’re honest about what we see and clear about what it means for you.
What makes us different
Not a black box.

You see the work as it happens, you understand the decisions behind it, and you can take it in-house the day we’re done.

Not a meeting machine.

We focus on decisions and outcomes, not unnecessary calls. Your time is part of the budget too.

Not a stranger by month two.

The team you start with is the team you finish with. No rotating account leads, no bait-and-switch.

Our Process

How an engagement works.

Every engagement is different. But the shape of how we work is consistent — because good process isn’t bureaucracy, it’s how you make sure nothing important gets skipped.

01

Discovery

We start by listening. Before we recommend anything, we need to understand your situation — your goals, your constraints, your team, and what you’ve already tried.

02

Definition

We align on scope, success criteria, and deliverables. You know exactly what we’re building together and how we’ll know it worked.

03

Execution

We do the work — collaboratively, transparently, and with regular check-ins. No surprises at delivery. No deliverables that land without context.

04

Handoff

We build for your ownership from day one. When the engagement ends, you have the work, the documentation, and the knowledge to run with it independently.
