About The Main Sequence

Experienced people. No unnecessary overhead.

We are a small, senior consulting practice built on the belief that the best work comes from clarity of purpose, depth of experience, and a genuine commitment to client outcomes.

A different kind of consulting firm.

Most consulting firms sell you a team. We sell you outcomes. There are no junior associates learning on your project, no bloated decks designed to justify retainers, and no advice that disappears when the engagement ends.

The Main Sequence was founded to do fewer things, better — with the kind of senior attention that most firms reserve for their biggest clients. Whether you’re a growing organization trying to get your story straight or an established one trying to close the gap between where you are and where you need to be, we’re built for that work.

We’re based in Chicago. We work with clients nationally.

How We Work

What you can count on.

Every engagement is shaped by four principles that govern how we show up and what we deliver.

01
Purpose
Every engagement starts with your goals, not ours. We ground the work in a clear understanding of your mission, your audience, and what success actually looks like for your organization — before we recommend anything.
02
Partnership
We work alongside your team, not above it. You get shared ownership and honest collaboration throughout — not a deliverable dropped on your desk at the end of an engagement you weren’t part of.
03
Performance
We measure what matters. Strategy that can’t be evaluated isn’t strategy — it’s conjecture. Everything we build is designed to perform and be held accountable to real outcomes.
04
Perspective
Years of cross-sector experience means we see patterns your team might miss. We bring an outside view — and we’re not afraid to say what we see, even when it’s not what you expected to hear.
